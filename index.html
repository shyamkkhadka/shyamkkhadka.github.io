News Publications

I have joined PhD in March 2023. I am advised by Cristian Hesselman, Ralph Holz, and Suzan Bayhan. I started my professional career as a Ruby on Rails developer in 2009 in Cloudfactory . After a few years, I joined Nepal Telecom where I gained experience working in different roles as a Software engineer, IP network engineer, System administrator, Senior telecom engineer until February 2023.

My research focuses on assessing the security properties of Internet paths and use the security properties in routing decisions.
